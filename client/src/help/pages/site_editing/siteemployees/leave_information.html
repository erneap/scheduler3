Employee's Leave Information

(Site Scheduler/Leadership only)

The Employee will take vacation, holidays or other types of leave from time to time and the companies normally require this tracked to allow the site leadership to advise the employee when they need to take or can't take any more vacation or holidays. The leave information tab provides displays and views for editing this information. It is divided into four sub-views:

  1. PTO/Holiday Display - a quick view of the employee's Holiday and leave use. Holidays are only displayed for companies that designate holidays for use. The leaves portion will break out any leave type (other than holidays) and display them by month. For PTO, or vacation, this is displayed as black lettering for actual dates taken or blue for scheduled dates. At the bottom, there may be a summary of PTO/Vacation and holidays with the purpose to give a balance the employees possesses for the year selected. You can view a different year by clicking the << or >> at the top of the display to adjust the displayed year backward or forward one year. PTO/Holiday View Help
  2. Leaves Display - This is provided to actually edit a single day of leave or holiday for the employee. Most leaves and holidays are scheduled and approved using the employee's leave request view, but there are times you will need to edit their leave manually, this is the tool to use. It will be explained in another help page.
  3. Leave Balance Display - This view is provided to input the employee's annual leave allocation plus the amount of leave they are bringing into the new calendar year from the old. It will be explained in Leave Balance help page.
  4. Lastly is the Leave Request Display. It functions exactly like your own Leave Request editor, except as an approver, you can create, edit, delete and approve leave requests. Leave Request Help
