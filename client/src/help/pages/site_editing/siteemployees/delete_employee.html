Deleting an Employee

(Site Scheduler/Leadership only)

Deleting an employee is a two-step process. You select the employee from the list in the editor view. This will bring up their information but we need to view their profile (name information) to delete them.

  1. Click the delete button in the upper-right side. A dialog will open to allow you to verify the deletion.
  2. Click the "Yes" button to delete or "No" to stop the process.
