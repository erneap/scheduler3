Editing Employee's Company Information/Deleting

(Site Scheduler/Leadership only)

  1. Click on the "Personal/Company" tab at the top of the view
  2. Click on the "Company Information" tab below the previous.
  3. Selectt the employee's company of assignment
  4. As needed, type in the other information

Company information includes all the person's company-related data. This includes the company from the contract list, the company assigned employee identifier and any additional company identifier (or just type in the employee identifier again), their respective job title, any rank or grade information, a cost center and/or division within the company.

NOTE: The employee's company assigned identifier and alternate identifier are important if the company is allowing the program to ingest timecard information. The identifiers are the used to corrolate the timecard data to the individual.

NOTE: All the fields are free text and optional, except for the company, so type in each field, as necessary.
