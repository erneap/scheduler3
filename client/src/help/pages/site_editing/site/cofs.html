Site Editing Overview

(Site Scheduler/Leadership only)

The site is the local grouping of employees associated with a mission or missions.

  1. It is normally lead by a single individual designated by the lead company and responsible to the company contract leadership for the accomplishment of the objective.
  2. Site will normally have at least one workcenter in which employees are scheduled to work. Workcenter's can, in turn, be populated with positions and/or shifts for work assignment.
  3. The site is also the level where two of the report types are defined. These two report types are the local charge number status and the certificate of service reports. They will both be described and the process for creation will be explained in later portions of this tutorial.
