Viewing the Site Schedule

The site schedule view is made up of two portions: the schedule and the legend. The legend portion show the various color coded schemes and their respective meanings within the schedule. These color schemes don't include any code where the background and text are displayed for normal work (white background with black lettering).

  1. The schedule portion provides the complete monthly schedule for the site, by work center, then by shift within the work center. The example shown above shows the month of June 2023, with three work centers (Leads, GEOINT, and HB/LB).
  2. Adjust the Month Shown - at the top of the schedule are four red buttons to adjust the month shown.
    • "<<" button will move the display backward one year.
    • "<" button will move the display backward one month.
    • ">" button will move the display forward one month.
    • ">>" button will move the display forward one year.
