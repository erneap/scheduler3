Viewing the Site Shift Coverage

(Site Supervisor View Only)

The site shift coverage view is a companion to the site schedule, showing the number of employees working on the shifts for those work centers with shifts. This helps the supervisor ensure enough people are scheduled for a particular shift. The minimum number of workers per shift is defined in the site editor (discussed later) as part of the shift definition. If a shift is below defined minimums, it is highlighted with a pink background.

  1. Adjust the Month Shown - at the top of the view are four red buttons to adjust the month shown.
    • "<<" button will move the display backward one year.
    • "<" button will move the display backward one month.
    • ">" button will move the display forward one month.
    • ">>" button will move the display forward one year.
